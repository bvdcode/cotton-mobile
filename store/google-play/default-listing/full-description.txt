Cotton Cloud for Android lets you connect to your Cotton Cloud instance from a native mobile app.

This first mobile release focuses on the foundation for a safe sign-in flow:

- Enter your Cotton Cloud instance URL.
- Approve access in your browser.
- Return to the app with your session stored in Android secure storage.
- Restore your session when the app starts again.
- Preview the signed-in account and instance.
- Sign out and clear the local session when needed.

The app is currently prepared for internal testing while the mobile experience grows from sign-in and account preview into richer Cotton Cloud workflows.
